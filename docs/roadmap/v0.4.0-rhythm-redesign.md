## Scope Reset

v0.4.0 is no longer a consonance-first alpha with rhythm documented as a
future-ready foundation. The release must demonstrate rhythm as a core part of
the same living musical system as Consonance Field, Density, Movement,
Viability, and Respawn.

The release may take longer. The bar is now:

- rhythm is composer-facing, not only an implementation detail
- rhythm and harmony interact in one ecology
- rhythm can be heard as musical structure without prose explanation
- three rhythm families are represented by curated demos
- a flagship demo shows rhythm, consonance, and life processes together

## Current Architecture Audit

| Surface | Current role | Gap |
|---|---|---|
| `src/life/scenario.rs` `WhenSpec` / `DurationSpec` | Public script model for `once`, `pulse`, `gates`, and field duration. | Timing is still expressed mostly as pulse scheduling plus duration. It does not name metric beat, entrained beat, or flow timing as distinct musical concepts. |
| `src/life/phonation_engine.rs` | Converts timing specs into tone on/off/update commands, theta gates, and weighted candidates. | Strong event machinery exists, but the musical rhythm model is implicit and hard to audition directly. |
| `src/life/articulation_core.rs` `KuramotoCore` | Contains endogenous phase, coupling, energy, reward, vitality, and 1/f phase noise. | This is close to agent-phase entrainment, but Rhai exposes it through low-level controls rather than a coherent entrainment surface. |
| `src/life/population/social.rs` / `src/life/social_density.rs` | Builds onset density traces and social coupling input. | Social timing is per-frame support, not yet a persistent rhythm field that can be shaped, inspected, or composed. |
| `ScaffoldConfig` and `set_scaffold_*` | External timing comparison controls. | Useful research controls, but not the final rhythm-composition API. |
| `TimbreGenotype::jitter` and sound-body pink noise | 1/f noise exists for timbral/pitch fluctuation. | Flow timing is not yet exposed as onset/rate structure. |
| Rhai rhythm API: `repeat`, `pulse`, `gates`, `sync`, `social`, `rhythm_freq`, `rhythm_coupling_vitality`, `rhythm_reward` | Enough to build first sketches. | Names are mechanism-level; they do not yet guide a composer toward beat, emergent synchronization, or flow timing patterns. |

## Rhythm Families Required For v0.4.0

### 1. Metric Beat

Purpose: provide an immediately legible Western-music-like pulse surface.

Expected behavior:

- stable beat or subdivision grid
- clear attack/duration roles
- optional offbeats or cross-pulses
- easy integration with consonance placement

Likely implementation path:

- keep `repeat`, `pulse`, `gates` working
- add or document higher-level beat presets only if they remove real script
  boilerplate
- verify onset timing with beat stability and inter-onset interval reports

Candidate demo:

- `samples/04_ecosystems/metric_beat_foundation.rhai`

### 2. Entrained Beat

Purpose: make beat emerge from living agents instead of an external metronome.

Expected behavior:

- agents have endogenous phase
- coupling, vitality, reward, and social density make synchronization audible
- weak or unsupported timing patterns decay
- stable pulse appears as a population-level event

Likely implementation path:

- promote `KuramotoCore` behavior into a clearer entrainment surface
- decide whether social density should become a persistent `RhythmField`
- make rhythm reward and metabolism legible in reports and demos
- avoid treating scaffold controls as the primary API

Candidate demo:

- `samples/04_ecosystems/entrained_beat.rhai`

### 3. Flow Timing

Purpose: make non-metric but structured timing audible, like flowing water,
firefly flashes, rain drops, or wind-driven clusters.

Expected behavior:

- not locked to a Western beat grid
- clustered but not random
- reproducible by seed
- measurable with burstiness and slope diagnostics
- usable with field/density/harmony processes

Likely implementation path:

- add a timing-level stationary renewal driver with local jitter and short
  rain-like clusters rather than reusing only timbral jitter or slow rate
  warping
- expose it through a small composer-facing Rhai surface
- provide headless metrics for onset distribution and approximate spectral
  slope

Candidate demo:

- `samples/04_ecosystems/flow_timing_field.rhai`

## Integration Flagship

The final v0.4.0 flagship should not be "harmony demo plus beat". It should
show one ecology in which:

- consonance field shapes viable pitch regions
- rhythm field or endogenous timing shapes viable onset regions
- agents move, sound, synchronize, survive, and respawn
- field changes alter both pitch organization and timing organization
- the result is musically legible without paper context

Candidate demo:

- `samples/04_ecosystems/conchordal_ecology.rhai`

## Measurement And Reports

Before auditioning the redesigned demos, add or extend headless reports for:

- onset count and onset density
- inter-onset interval distribution
- beat stability for metric patterns
- Kuramoto order or phase-locking for entrained beat
- synchronization emergence time
- burstiness for flow patterns
- approximate onset-density and IOI-sequence 1/f slopes for flow timing

These reports do not replace audition, but they prevent rhythm demos from
passing only because the prose explains them.
