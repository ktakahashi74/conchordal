Conchordal is a bio-mimetic ecosystem where cognition becomes terrain, and sounds resonate—not as symbols, but as life that breathes.

Why that sound, there? We reclaim the lost gravity of music.

Conchordal is an open-source instrument for generative composition, grounded
in psychoacoustics. Instead of placing notes on a grid, autonomous sound agents
find their own consonance on a perceptual landscape. Written in Rust, it
provides an intuitive scripting interface in Rhai.
